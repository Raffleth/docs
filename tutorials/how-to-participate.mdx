description: Learn how you can join an existing raffle.
---

To participate in a raffle using Raffl, follow these steps:

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect your wallet">
Visit the Raffl website (https://raffl.xyz) and connect your web3 wallet.
</Step>

<Step title="Go to the raffle page">
Head to the page of the raffle you want to participate. If you don't the raffle url, you can also search the raffle with the search bar.
</Step>

<Step title="Purchase the amount of entries you want">
- Click on the "Buy entries" button.
- Specify the amount of entries you want to purchase
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm the transaction">
Confirm the transaction with your web3 wallet
</Step>

<Step title="Done!">
Awesome! ðŸ˜Ž You are now participating in the raffle.
</Step>

</Steps>

<Note>
After participating in a raffle, **take into account** the following:

- The raffle will be made automatically if it has reached at least to the minimum required entries.
- If the raffle is cancelled due to not meeting the minimum entries requirement, a window will open for users to withdraw their entries with no penalties.
</Note>
