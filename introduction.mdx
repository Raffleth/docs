Whether you're a user looking to participate in a raffle, a creator keen on launching a unique lottery, or a developer planning to integrate Raffl into your protocol, you'll find the information you need right here. This comprehensive guide will walk you through the process of setting up and managing your raffles, navigating our platform, and even diving deep into the technical aspects if you so wish!

Here's what you can expect to find:

- **How-To Guides:** Step-by-step instructions on how to create raffles, participate in raffles, claim prizes, and more.
- **Feature Overview:** Detailed insights into our range of features and how they can enhance your raffle experience.
- **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):** Answers to common questions about our platform.
- **Integration Guide:** Detailed instructions on how to integrate Raffl into your own protocol.
- **Contact Information:** Details on how to get in touch with us for support, partnership inquiries, or anything else you might need.

We've designed this documentation to be as clear and straightforward as possible, but if you can't find what you're looking for, don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help!

We're excited to see what you create with Raffl. Happy raffling!
